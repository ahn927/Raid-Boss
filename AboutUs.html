About Us

What is Tap'N Cook?

Tap'N Cook is a web app designed to reduce food waste.
The app's purpose follows the Love Food Hate Waste campaign
that was initiated by Metro Vancouver.

How?

By selecting up to four ingredents that you have at home, the app will suggest various nutrious yet simple recipes using these ingredients.

Full recipe instructions, along with dietary restrictions and cooking times, are provided for your convinence.

You never have to go to another recipe website again!
