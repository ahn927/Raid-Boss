About Us

What is Tap'N Cook?

Tap'N Cook is a web app designed to reduce food waste.
The app's purpose follows the Love Food Hate Waste campaign that was initiated by Metro Vancouver.

How?

By selecting up to five ingredents that you have at home, the app will suggest various nutrious yet simple recipes using these ingredients.

Full recipe instructions, along with dietary restrictions and cooking times, are provided for your convinence.

You never have to go to another recipe website again!

Our Advantage

Unlike other Recipe Apps out there that limits you to select from their own small list of ingredients, and finds recipes from their own small list of recipes, we have taken advantage of Spoonacular's API to provide you with over 360,000 recipes and 2,600 ingredients to choose from, with daily additions. We then filter these recipes to carefully meet your needs, which ensures that you will find numerous relevant recipes for any ingredient you may have left in your fridge. In addition, unlike other Recipe Apps that redirect you to other websites, we made it easy for you by providing all the recipe instructions and information you need in our website, so you never have to visit any other recipe website ever again!
