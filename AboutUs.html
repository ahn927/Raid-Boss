Tap'N CooK

About us:

Tap'N Cook is a web app that is optimized for mobile devices. The app's purpose follows the Love Food Hate Waste campaign that was initiated by Metro Vancouver

The app simply prompts users for input, the input is the user's almost-expiring food. Based on user's input the app will recommend simple, nutricious recipes to make use of the provided ingredients as opposed to throwing them away.

Our goals are:

  1. Reduce food waste
  2. Promote green environment
  3. Encourage healthier lifestyle
